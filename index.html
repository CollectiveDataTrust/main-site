Why

Your data is your asset, because it has value and is used in transactions that moves the information economy.

You are the reason for the existence of internet. You are the reason for the existence of machines. You should have a say on the evolution of both.

This is not about the money being made of your data with no benefit for you. This is about your importance when it comes to the decisions that affects you as the user.

We can not expect that all users will give up to their lifestyle and needs, just to protect their data. The only way to deal with this, is putting you - the owner of the data - in full control of it.

This is not an issue of policies and regulations, this is an issue of CONTROL and OWNERSHIP

What

This is The Collective Data Trust.

We are a community of internet users where we care so much about Artificial Intelligence development, and Data Monetization practices, that we have created the Collective Data Trust (CDT).

Collective Data Trust allows any internet user to become a Beneficiary of the Trust by signing in. Once registered, the Beneficiary becomes owner of the company that monetizes his/her data. The Trust allows its Beneficiaries to take advantage from any monetization process that occurs involving his data while having full control about which data is to be used, how, it will be used and by whom.

The Beneficiary's data is used to settle each account, creating a Data-as-an-asset portfolio through the strategic implementation of a collaborative model for the development of Artificial Intelligence and Collective Data Monetization.

Collective Data Trust is a fiduciary entity, legally established under the State of Delaware, with registration No. 6855308, and by which, once you sign in, you become a beneficiary, and you are entitled to ownership over the trust assets, proportionally to your own asset integration. By becoming a Beneficiary of the Trust, the USER is turned into an OWNER

Each Beneficiary will own a part of a set of companies without paying any money.

Each Beneficiary will select which data to integrate and how it will be used.

Each Beneficiary entitled the the Trust with the mission to use his data to create and capture value.

Such value is used by the Trust to generate profit for you.

How

Imagine that someone could pay you just for existing, and sharing some information about your existence

CDT looks to contribute with a global passive income, but instead of hopping for some social justice or claiming over economics rights, we take the system as it is, and we look for ways to make it happen. So we focus on the possibility that Data has to produce income to its owners.

By signing in, you become owner of the company that monetizes your data.

Instead of investing money or any other financial assets for your shares, you are integrating your data as an asset, that will be collectively monetized along with the data of all the other beneficiaries.

By Monetization we refer to processes aligned to our mission, that will create and increase the use value or the exchange value of the collective data, either through products development in the field of Artificial Intelligence, or through direct monetization and applied services like marketing and research.

Once you have signed in, you are no longer just an user, you are the owner.

The kind of assets that the user contributes as settlement to become a beneficiary is not his money, but his data

Once the owners accumulates a critical mass of data into the collective digital assets, the Trust, can optimize monetization process and start giving tangible results to the owners.

At the same time, this mass of data will be used to train and enhance artificial intelligence powered services and products, that will be at the service of the beneficiaries and under the individual control of each of them.

There are many ways you can benefit from the Trust, and since all benefits are based on the use of Data-as-an-Asset to create value, we refer to this processes as the Collective Data Monetization System, where we secure a private and safe environment for a user-based, data-centric distributed economy, beneficial to all its participants.

Each beneficiary can participate in different ways:

Passive monetization:

You choose what you will like to share, and we will create value out of it.

Active Monetization:

Teach the machine, answer paid questions, participate in rewarded research campaigns, and sell specifically demanded data, under your own terms.

The Collective Data Trust will execute work on 3 main missions:

Monetize and Increase the value of the collective data for the Beneficiaries, to provide them with a source of passive income, and the additional possibility to actively generate income through different business opportunities across the platform.

Democratize Artificial Intelligence resources access, providing each beneficiary with its own personal and private Artificial Intelligence development platform, and allowing the community to have a collective intelligence platform, owned by all the beneficiaries.

Advocate and Pursue a fair access to internet for the Beneficiaries, and represent the best interest of the community in all fields where possible.

WHY A TRUST?

For legal and customary reasons, a Trust is the perfect instrument to hold assets under a legal structure, and to hold those who manage the assets, accountable for management and allocation.

In this way, each beneficiary is owner of his part of the trust, which is proportional to his digital assets representation on the trust, and to how much this represents to the total digital assets of all the beneficiaries together.
